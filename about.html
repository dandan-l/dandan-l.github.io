  • A little about me

  • Hi, my name is Dan Liu. I am a creative UX designer who advocates for empowering and intuitive concepts with my diverse skills in design, research, and programming. Passionate about empathizing with users and incorporating their stories and needs, I have a long-established design and research history working closely with users, non-profit organizations, researchers, and developers. Currently, I am pursuing a master's degree at the University of Colorado Boulder, and working as a lead product designer for Peerbots and a web development intern for Celestial and Spaceflight Mechanics Lab.

  • I was born in Shenzhen, China, a place that is full of possibilities. My childhood involves a lot of moving and changes. This experience helps me build an easy adaptation to the new environment. At the same time, being frequently exposed to the new environment, I developed a strong curiosity about the world. Driven by this curiosity, I spent a lot of time traveling. I believe that I should be on adventures to embrace new places and cultures while I am still young. So far, I have visited China, the United States, Japan, Korea, Copenhagen, and Germany. During my trips, I had a lot of fun bus rides chatting with the bus drivers about local cultures.

  • Outside of work and school, I am a big fan of food. I enjoy trying out new cuisines and restaurants and cooking my food with 40% of recipe, 40% of imagination and 20% of excitement to share with friends and family.

  • I built this website with some help from my supportive friend, Casey Hunt, using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
